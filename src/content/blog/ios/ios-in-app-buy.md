### is_trial_period
An indicator of whether an auto-renewable subscription is in the free trial period.
指示自动续期订阅是否处于免费试用期的指标。
#### Possible Values
true
The subscription is in the free trial period.
订阅处于免费试用期。

false
The subscription is not in the free trial period.
订阅不在免费试用期内。
